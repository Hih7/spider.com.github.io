The SPIDR Tech Platform

Much of daily law enforcement is customer service: taking calls, informing citizens, conducting follow-ups and providing updates. With SPIDR Tech, you can take your customer service into the 21st century: through our secure, cloud-based, and scalable system, your agency can enhance citizen experience through automated emails and texts -- preserving staff time for best-in-class service delivery.
